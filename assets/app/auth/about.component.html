About the Project

Why I Made This?

My goal for this project was to: (1) learn Angular 2, and (2) create an app that would help a local business by improving one aspect of its experience.

What Does It Do?

This app was made to record haircut appointments for a local barber shop where customers primary make appointments by phone. As a customer, one of my least favorite part was making an appointment over the phone because it was unreliable or the person over the phone sounded rude. However, since I really liked my barber and getting my haircut there, I wanted to create an app that would benefit them.
